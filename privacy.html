Section 1

Information We Collect

When you use Soul Sync, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, or phone number (only if voluntarily provided during sign-up or SOS calls).
  • Chat Data: Messages exchanged with our chatbot to provide supportive responses. These chats are not shared with third parties.
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, and general usage data for performance improvements.
  • Camera Access(Face Detection): If you choose to use the face detection feature, video data is processed locally on your device and is not stored or transmitted.

Certain data is collected automatically via cookies, SDKs, or similar technologies. See Cookies & Tracking.

Section 2

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide safe and reliable support services.
  • Enable SOS features for contacting parents, guardians, or local authorities.
  • Improve app functionality and user experience.
  • Generate uplifting and positive blog content.

Section 3

How we protect your information

  • All sensitive data is encrypted and securely handled.
  • Chat conversations and camera data remain private and are not shared externally.
  • We follow industry-standard practices to safeguard your information.

Opt-out: You may opt out of certain tracking by using the “Do Not Track” settings in your browser or platform-level privacy controls where available.

Section 4

Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information.Information is only shared in the following cases:

  • With your explicit consent.
  • To comply with legal obligations or emergency situations.
  • If you choose to initiate SOS calls, your contact information will be shared directly with the selected recipient.
